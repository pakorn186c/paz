## EMB-LR1302-mPCI

EMB-LR1302-mPCIe provides long range connectivity using ultra-long range spread
spectrum communication and high interference immunity on the 868 (or 915 MHz)
MHz radio band. This device is characterized by far less power consumption than
previous solutions, has a better thermal design and it is capable of handling a
higher amount of traffic than preceding devices.
